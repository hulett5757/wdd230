Meet Tasha Smith

Name: Tasha
Gender: Female
Age: 30
Occupation: Business Annalyst

Tasha is the owner of Tooele Business Annalysts. She established her business here in Tooele in 2017. She helps other businesses in the area to evaluate their business model and progress, and devices a plan for them to improve their business model and increase profits. We appreciate all the hard work she has done in our community and with the Tooele Community Chamber of Commerce.

The purpose of Tooele Community Chamber of Commerce is to provide businesses and the people living in our community with resources to help them interact and facilitate our community to be healthy and thrive.
It is the mission of the chamber of commerce to be a catalyst for business growth and development.
