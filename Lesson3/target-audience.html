Target Audience

The Target audience for this website is business owners in our community and people living in, interested in moving to, or visiting our community. They will be from 18 to 100 years old. They will be interested in getting involved in our community and making it a better place to live. They will have the technological capability to access a webiste and navigate a menu. This website will be focused to help all income levels in the community. As stated in our purpose for this website,we are here to provide businesses and the people in our community with resources to help them interact and facilitate our community to be healthy and thrive."
